1) Summary (No personal data collected)

HOTmate (“the App”) works as an offline calculator/reference tool.

We do not collect, store, transmit, sell, or share personal data from within the App. This includes (for example) name, email, phone number, precise location, contacts, photos, microphone recordings, advertising identifiers (IDFA), analytics events, usage profiles, or crash reports sent to third parties.

No tracking / no analytics / no ads:

  • We do not use analytics SDKs.
  • We do not use advertising SDKs.
  • We do not perform cross-app tracking.
  • We do not integrate third-party crash reporting.
  • We do not build user profiles or behavioral history.

How the App works: calculations are performed locally on your device. Any data used to generate results stays on your device unless you choose to share it yourself (for example, by taking a screenshot).

2) Information stored on your device (local-only)

For convenience and usability, the App may store local-only information on your device, such as:

This local data:

How to delete local data: uninstall the App (and/or clear the App’s storage via iOS settings where available).

Device backups (your choice): depending on your iOS settings, local App data may be included in device backups (e.g., iCloud backups). These backups are controlled by you and Apple; we do not have access to them.
